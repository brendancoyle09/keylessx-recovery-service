Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cryptocurrencies can you recover?

We specialize in the recovery of various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT, and many other digital assets lost due to scams, hacks, or inaccessible wallets.

How long does the recovery process take?

The duration of the recovery process varies depending on the complexity of the case. While some cases can be resolved within days, others might take a few weeks. We strive for the fastest possible recovery while ensuring thoroughness and security.

Is your service guaranteed?

While we cannot guarantee 100% recovery in every case (as some funds may be untraceable or irretrievable), we commit to a no-stone-unturned approach. We only take on cases where we believe there is a strong possibility of success, and we keep you informed throughout the entire process.

What information do you need to start a recovery?

To begin, we'll need details about how your funds were lost, transaction IDs, wallet addresses involved, any communications with scammers, and other relevant information. All data is handled with the utmost confidentiality and security.
