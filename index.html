Frequently Asked Questions

Is your service truly confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is our top priority. We operate under strict non-disclosure agreements and employ military-grade security protocols to ensure your data and case details are completely protected.

What is your success fee?

We work on a "no recovery, no fee" basis. Our fee is a percentage of the recovered assets, which is determined on a case-by-case basis and is only due upon successful recovery. The initial assessment is always free.

How long does the recovery process take?

The timeline varies greatly depending on the complexity of the case. A simple password recovery may take a few days, while a complex blockchain investigation could take several weeks or months. We will provide an estimated timeline during your initial assessment.
